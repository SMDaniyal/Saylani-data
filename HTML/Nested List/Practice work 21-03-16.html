Pizza Recipe

I love going to the Otto pizzeria in New York City for their delicious pizzas. One of their pizzas is a mushroom pizza with taleggio cheese. I wanted to create my own version of it using puff pastry instead of regular pizza dough. I also wanted to create a puff pastry pizza that is a twist on pissaladiere. However, Instead of using black olives I wanted to add some Indian flavor.

Ingridients
  • Taleggio cheese (or any of your favorite cheeses!)
  • About 2 handfuls of mixed mushrooms
    • (cleaned and chopped bite size)
  • A handful of fresh parsley, roughly chopped
  • Puff Pastry (1 box, thawed)
  • Chili powder (or Paprika)
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
